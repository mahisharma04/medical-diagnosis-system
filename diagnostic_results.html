Common Cold

Likelihood: Medium Probability

The common cold is a viral infection of your nose and throat. Symptoms usually include runny nose, sore throat, and cough.

Next Steps: Rest, stay hydrated, and monitor symptoms. See a doctor if symptoms worsen.

Flu (Influenza)

Likelihood: Low Probability

The flu is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It can cause mild to severe symptoms, including fever and body aches.

Next Steps: Consult a healthcare provider for further guidance.
